# THE PLAN

## Dynamic Routing

An algorithm that gathers real time GTFS data as input focusing on underserviced communities where demand exceeds capacity. Our algorithm processes this in real time, and is designed to log and communicate patterns of disservice to more flexible transit vehicles, to attempt to route them to lift the demand on those communities. The output will be our findings communicated to DRT.

## Passenger Signaling

**weighted average that alters the dynamic routing**
An app that pulls a users location as input, processes it by comparing it to the static GTFS stop locations, and if they match geographically then it communicates a signal that a potential rider is present. Output is shifting the weight of the Dynamic Routing
